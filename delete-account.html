Delete Your Account & Data

Effective date: [2026-07-18]

This page explains how to request deletion of your account and data associated with KeepUp!, developed by Invictus Spark.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to cramuir@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request", including:

  • The platform you play on (Android or iOS)
  • Your Play Games / Game Center display name (if known)
  • The approximate date you last played, to help us locate your data

We will process your request and confirm deletion within a reasonable timeframe, generally within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted

KeepUp! does not operate its own account system or backend server. We do not store a separate database of player accounts. Data associated with your play is held in two places:

Data we can delete on request

  • Any locally cached identifiers we may retain for support/troubleshooting purposes tied to reports you've sent us (e.g., correspondence, bug reports)
  • Any record of your in-game progress if it has ever been shared with us directly (e.g., attached to a support request)

Data managed by Google, not by us

Because sign-in, leaderboards, and purchases run through Google's own services, some data is stored and controlled directly by Google rather than by Invictus Spark:

  • Play Games Services (leaderboard scores, achievements, player ID) — manage or delete this directly through your Google Account under Data & Privacy settings, or via the Play Games app.
  • Advertising ID — reset or opt out of ad personalization anytime in your device's system settings, or via Google's Ads Settings.
  • Purchase history — retained by Google Play Billing as part of your Google Account's transaction records, viewable and manageable at Google Play's account page.

We recommend requesting deletion through us and reviewing your Google Account privacy settings, since some data is jointly held between the two.

Data Retention

We do not retain gameplay data beyond what is described above. Any data tied to a support request is kept only as long as needed to resolve that request, and is deleted upon your request or after a reasonable retention period.

Questions

If you have any questions about this process, contact us at cramuir@gmail.com.
