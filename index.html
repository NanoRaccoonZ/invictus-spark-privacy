Privacy Policy

Effective date: [2026-07-18]

This privacy policy describes how Invictus Spark ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information in connection with KeepUp! (the "Game"), a mobile application available on Google Play and the App Store.

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect or store personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number. However, the Game integrates third-party services that may collect certain information automatically:

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The Game displays advertisements and offers rewarded ads (e.g., for extra attempts) via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (a resettable, non-permanent device identifier)
  • Device information (device model, operating system, language)
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, ad performance)

This data is used to serve and measure advertising. Ad personalization is only applied to users who have passed the Game's age screen and are not identified as being under the applicable age of consent; see Section 4 for details on how younger users are handled differently. You can review or reset your advertising ID in your device's privacy settings, and manage ad personalization through Google's Ads Settings.

Google Play Games Services

If you sign in with Google Play Games, the Game accesses your Play Games player ID to enable:

  • Leaderboards (submitting and displaying your score)
  • Achievements (if applicable)
  • Cloud save / cross-device progress (if applicable)

This data is managed under Google's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

Purchases of in-game currency or items are processed entirely through Google Play Billing / Apple's In-App Purchase system. We do not receive or store your payment card details. We may receive confirmation of purchase (product ID, transaction status) solely to grant the corresponding in-game item.

Crash and Performance Data

The Game may use standard platform-level crash reporting (e.g., Google Play Console vitals) to identify and fix technical issues. This data is aggregated and not used to identify individual users.

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate core game features (leaderboards, purchases, rewarded ads)
  • To display and measure advertising
  • To diagnose crashes and improve stability and performance
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Third-Party Services

The Game relies on the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play Games Services — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play Billing — policies.google.com/privacy

4. Children's Privacy

The Game is intended for a general, mixed-age audience and may be accessed by children. In accordance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and Google Play's Families Policy Requirements, we take the following measures:

  • The Game includes a neutral age screen. We do not collect or store the exact birthdate entered; it is used only to determine an age bracket for ad-serving purposes.
  • Users identified as under the applicable age of consent (13 in the United States, or a higher age of digital consent in certain other jurisdictions) are served non-personalized advertising only. Persistent identifiers (such as advertising ID) are not used to build behavioral profiles for these users.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond what is strictly necessary for the Game to function (e.g., an anonymous, non-personalized ad request).
  • Advertising to users in this category is served only through ad SDKs and mediation partners certified for use with Google Play's Families program.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information beyond what is described above, please contact us using the details below so we can investigate and remove it.

5. Your Choices

  • You can reset or limit your device's advertising ID in system settings.
  • You can manage or revoke Google Play Games Services permissions via your Google Account settings.
  • You can request account/progress data deletion by contacting us below.

6. Data Retention

Leaderboard and progress data tied to your Play Games account is retained as long as your account is active with the Game. You may request deletion at any time.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
